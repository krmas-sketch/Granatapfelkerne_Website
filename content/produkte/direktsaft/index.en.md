### 100% Pure NFC Juice with No Additives

Our pomegranate Not-From-Concentrate (NFC) juice is cold-pressed exclusively from the arils of the finest sun-ripened fruits. This gentle pressing method ensures that the valuable polyphenols, vitamins, and intense ruby-red color are fully preserved.

**B2B Applications:**
As a premium raw ingredient, our direct juice is highly sought after in the beverage industry (smoothies, mixed juices), the production of dietary supplements, and fine dining. We completely avoid added sugars, preservatives, or artificial flavors.

**Supply Formats:**
- Bottled for retail resale.
- Bag-in-Box or IBC containers for industrial processing.
