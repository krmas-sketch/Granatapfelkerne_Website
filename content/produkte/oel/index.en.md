### Liquid Gold for Cosmetics and Health

Our pomegranate seed oil is a highly pure, cold-pressed premium oil extracted from the dried seeds of the fruit. It is one of the few plant oils containing a high concentration of punicic acid (an Omega-5 fatty acid).

**Industrial Applications:**
Due to its strong antioxidant and cell-regenerating properties, our oil is a sought-after raw material in natural cosmetics (anti-aging creams, serums), as well as in the pharmaceutical industry and for dietary supplements.

**Quality Guarantee:**
- **Cold-Pressed:** 100% natural and solvent-free.
- **Premium Quality:** Highest purity backed by rigorous laboratory analysis.
- **Bulk Packaging:** Available in drums and IBC containers for industrial processors.
